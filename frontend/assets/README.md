Drop a short (5-15 second) sample bird-call clip here as `demo-clip.wav`,
and a matching `demo-clip-info.json`:

{"audio_url": "demo-clip.wav"}

This backs the "Try identification demo" button — useful for your video
if you want one guaranteed-good identification on hand as a fallback,
in addition to recording/uploading something live. A good source for a
demo clip you have clear rights to use: record your own, or download one
from xeno-canto.org under its listed license and credit the recordist.
